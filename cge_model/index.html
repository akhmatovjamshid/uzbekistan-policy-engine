🛡️
Import Tariff +5pp
Raise import tariff rate from 2% to 7%. Evaluates protectionist trade policy impact on domestic output, exchange rate, and absorption.
📉
Export Price Fall −10%
World export price falls 10% (e.g. gold price shock). Simulates terms-of-trade deterioration and balance of payments adjustment.
⛽
World Import Price +10%
Rise in world import prices +10% (energy/food global shock). Tests real exchange rate and welfare effects of a terms-of-trade deterioration.
🏛️
Energy Reform: VAT +5pp
Indirect tax rate rises from 6% to 11% (VAT broadening). Evaluates fiscal revenue impact and absorption effects of energy subsidy reform.
💰
FDI Surge: Foreign Savings +50%
Foreign savings (FDI inflows) increase 50%. Assesses Dutch disease risk, exchange rate appreciation, and investment expansion.
✂️
Government Austerity −20%
Government consumption cut by 20%. Fiscal consolidation scenario — evaluates GDP multiplier, trade balance, and private sector crowding-in.
👷
Labor Supply Shock +10%
Labor force grows 10% (e.g. rural-urban migration, returning diaspora). Tests unemployment absorption, wage pressure, and formality dynamics.
📋
Minimum Wage: Steep φ = 1.2
High wage elasticity (φ = 1.2) simulates binding minimum wage effect — wages rise steeply with employment, slowing job creation. Tests employment-wage trade-off.
