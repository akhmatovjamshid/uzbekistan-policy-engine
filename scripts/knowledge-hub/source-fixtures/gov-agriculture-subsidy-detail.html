Financing and subsidy plans for the agricultural sector reviewed

2025-12-09 17:05:00

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev reviewed a presentation on the effective organization of financing for cotton and grain cultivation, as well as a new subsidy system for the agriculture complex.

A total of 34.2 trillion soums is planned for the cotton and grain harvest, subsidies, and other expenses next year. Additionally, there are plans to attract an extra 200 million dollars to purchase 800 cotton harvesting machines.

It was proposed to allocate an additional 5 trillion soums in 2026 for agrotechnical measures, including expenses for wrap and pipes.

In accordance with the Decree of the President of Uzbekistan on measures for the fundamental improvement of the state support system for the agricultural sector, the Agricultural Payments Agency has been established under the Ministry of Agriculture.

Currently, there are 52 types of subsidies in agriculture. Under the new system, these processes will be fundamentally simplified, application reviews will be fully digitized, and all procedures will be conducted through Agroportal.

Subsidies worth 1.3 trillion soums allocated for 2026 will be provided proactively by government agencies without requiring applicant involvement, and the remainder will be disbursed through a simplified procedure through Agrosubsidy.
