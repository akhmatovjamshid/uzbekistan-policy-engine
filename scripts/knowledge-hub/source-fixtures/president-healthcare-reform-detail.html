1 May 2026

Healthcare system development issues discussed

The official presidential source describes approved measures and instructions for healthcare reform, including revised licensing requirements for medical organizations from July 1, 2026, accreditation rules for state-funded services, mandatory licensing deadlines for state medical institutions, and a private-sector participation package.

The measures include preferential credit resources for new clinics, loans for investment projects, social tax and customs exemptions, and an agency for medical investment and public-private partnership project implementation.
