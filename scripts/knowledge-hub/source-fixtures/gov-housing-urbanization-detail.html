Progress and priorities in housing construction and urbanization reviewed

2026-04-15 09:10:00

On April 14, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev chaired a meeting to review progress and outline priority tasks in housing construction and urbanization.

Regional khokims and the National Committee for Urbanization were instructed to complete the development of master plans for 6 cities and 44 towns, as well as for 104 villages using budget funds this year, and to begin preparing urban planning documentation for a further 50 settlements in cooperation with private investors.

Each regional khokim was instructed to develop comprehensive programs for the implementation of approved master plans for regional and district centers within two months.

It was decided to transition within three months to a system that will process land privatization applications online within 5-10 minutes without human intervention.

From July 1, the combined design permit and architectural and urban planning assignment practice will be extended to all regions. From July 1, technical specifications for electricity, gas, water supply, and sewerage will be issued through a single application and a single payment.

Responsible officials were instructed to submit a draft resolution on reducing requirements, timelines, and payments by at least half within three months. From June 1, the performance of state clients will be evaluated based on KPIs.

This year, multi-apartment residential buildings with 140,000 apartments must be commissioned in the regions.
