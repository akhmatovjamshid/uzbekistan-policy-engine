What this proves

A single policy shock — a tariff increase on chemicals & plastics (HS 28–40) — flows through four economic models simultaneously. All four models pull their structural parameters from shared/parameter-registry.json, so a change in one place propagates everywhere. The Reconciliation Table at the bottom flags cross-model disagreement.

Integration topology: PE computes direct trade diversion → IO propagates the import-cost shock through 136 sectors → CGE reallocates resources in general equilibrium → FPP projects fiscal revenue & external accounts.
